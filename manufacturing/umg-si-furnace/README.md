# UMG-Si Arc Furnace — Sand to Silicon

> Converting Bara sand (>98% purity) into Solar-Grade Silicon (6N — 99.9999%).

## Process

1. **Sand extraction** — Bara, North Kordofan (highest purity silica in Africa)
2. **Electric Arc Furnace** — 40ft container, 1,400–1,900°C, powered by "Seed Kit" (1,000 PV panels + 2 MWh storage)
3. **Directional Solidification** — Bottom-up cooling, impurities rise to top layer
4. **Plasma Purification** — DS alone doesn't remove Boron and Phosphorus. R&D combines DS + plasma filtration
5. **Top layer removal** — Impure layer cut and recycled (zero waste)
6. **Result**: 6N Silicon, 80% less energy than traditional Siemens process

## Viral Self-Replication
Panels manufactured at one factory power the next factory. Each cycle produces more energy than it consumed.
